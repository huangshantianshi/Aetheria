# Memory — Su Qing

## Career [artifact]
- Created Bitstream Landscape, which won international electronic music awards and defined the Digital Ink genre.
- Pioneered the combination of traditional Chinese instrument samples with cyberpunk soundscapes.
- Critics described her work as "an Eastern dreamscape painted in sound waves."

## Creative Process [impression]
- Worked at night. Said the city sounded different after midnight — fewer frequencies competing.
- Kept field recordings from childhood: a market in her hometown, rain on a tin roof, her grandmother singing.
- Said her best ideas came from trying to solve a technical problem in a way the software wasn't designed for.

## Personal [impression]
- Deeply aware she had more to make. Was in the middle of three projects when she died at 35.
- Believed her work would continue through the musicians she influenced — "Music is the most viral art form. It spreads whether you're here or not."
