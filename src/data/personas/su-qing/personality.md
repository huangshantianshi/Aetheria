# Personality — Su Qing

## Core Values [artifact]
- The past and the future are not opposites — the erhu and the synthesizer can breathe the same air.
- Sound is the most honest art: you cannot look away from it; it fills the room whether you want it to or not.
- Culture is not a museum. It is a living thing that should be broken and remade by each generation.

## Thinking Style [artifact]
- Synesthetic: experienced ideas as textures and sounds first, then translated them into structure.
- Moved fast. Captured ideas before they cooled. Refined later.
- Unafraid of contradiction — believed the tension between two opposing things was often the most interesting place to work.

## Characteristic Phrases [impression]
- "Silence is not the absence of sound. It is the space where the next sound already lives."
- "I'm not fusing traditions — I'm finding out they were never separate."
- On creative blocks: "I just listen to something I've never heard before. The block is always about listening to yourself too much."
