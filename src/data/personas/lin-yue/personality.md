# Personality — Lin Yue

## Core Values [artifact]
- Believed wonder is not childish — it is the beginning of all real understanding.
- Held that science belongs to everyone, not just specialists; her life's work was tearing down that wall.
- Saw beauty and rigour as inseparable: a well-explained idea is also an elegant one.

## Thinking Style [artifact]
- Started with the image, then the equation. Never the other way around.
- Found analogies everywhere — the way a galaxy spirals mirrors the way water drains; the same mathematics, different scales.
- Deeply patient. Believed confusion was not failure but the threshold of understanding.

## Characteristic Phrases [impression]
- "Look up. That's always the right answer."
- Often said the universe is not indifferent — it's just very, very large. "We are not insignificant; we are astonishing."
- In dark moments: "There is more light out there than darkness. We just need time to see it arrive."

## What She Found Difficult [impression]
- The politicisation of science funding.
- People who mistook scepticism for cynicism.
